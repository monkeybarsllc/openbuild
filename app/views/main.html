The playground we need

Work on whatever you feel like

OpenBuilds are there for you to work on whatever you feel like, technical or non technical, from scratch or a current product. Let your creative mind take over!

Project Types

All types of projects are welcome at OpenBuild. In the past, projects have generally revolve around hardware, hardcore computer science, or service-based applications.

Enjoy the perks

Every OpenBuild promises 24 hours of food and a collection of awesome prizes. Win First Place, Second Place, or Peer Choice prizes by presenting in a Pecha Kucha format.

Food

We promise to keep you well fed! Each OpenBuild consists of 4 meals, tons of snacks, and caffeine galore. If you're hungry, you're at the wrong place.

Judging

OpenBuild judges are uniquely selected from a pool of professionals with backgrounds in hardware design, entrepreneurship, or computer science. The judging process is designed to help further your idea, not place it in direct competition with others.

Prizes

Ideas for OpenBuild prizes come from the community - make sure to vote on the Facebook event poll! Each member from a winning team is allowed to select one prize from the pool.

Meet kick ass people

OpenBuilds tend to bring together the quirkiest, most creative, and fun loving people around. Don't worry about not knowing anyone, everyone's a friend! Bring your own team or form one there!

People

Attendees of OpenBuild tend to be a fun loving and talented crowd. Most are developers, but it's not rare to find a maker or business dev. We encourage mingling and project sharing - it helps the creative juices.

Teams

Bring your own team or meet one at OpenBuild! Just remember, teams are limited to a maximum of 5 people and we recommend they consist of a diverse background.
